About Us

Learn more about our team and what drives us

Who We Are

We are a dedicated team of Computer Studies students from Tarlac State University, passionate about learning and applying cutting-edge technologies in web development. Our team was formed as part of the Integrative Programming Technology 2 (IPT2) course, where we aim to demonstrate our understanding of version control systems, collaborative development, and modern web design principles.

Through this project, we showcase not only our technical skills in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, but also our ability to work effectively as a team using Git and GitHub workflows. Each member brings unique perspectives and skills that contribute to our collective success.

Our Mission

To create innovative and user-friendly web solutions while continuously improving our technical skills and collaborative practices. We strive to deliver high-quality work that demonstrates our commitment to excellence in software development.

Our Vision

To become proficient developers who can contribute meaningfully to the tech industry. We envision a future where our skills in version control, web development, and teamwork enable us to tackle complex challenges and create impactful digital experiences.
